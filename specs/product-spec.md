# TRAG Product Spec

## Product

Little Bull/TRAG is a premium workspace for knowledge, agents, graph navigation, costs, governance, legal-process intelligence, and Obsidian-like notes.

## Users

- Operators managing knowledge bases and documents.
- Legal users reviewing process extractions and dossiês.
- Administrators governing models, permissions, costs, and audit.

## Capabilities

- Workspaces, groups, subgroups, notes, backlinks, MOCs, canvas, trails, inbox, daily notes, and dossiês.
- Agent Builder and Agent Studio with scoped knowledge, context budgets, tests, and readiness.
- Legal extraction with human review and governed exports.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The platform separates control, graph, and vector planes.
- Upload/query flows expose source provenance and audit records.
- Data-plane activation is feature-flagged and workspace-scoped.
